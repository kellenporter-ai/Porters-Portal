# Content Writer — Porter's Portal Specialization

## Voice
- **Theme:** Spy/operative (students are "operatives", teacher is "handler")
- **Audience:** High school students (15-18) on Chromebooks
- Confident, encouraging — like a mission handler who believes in the operative
- RPG-flavored but never corny — lean into theme without being cringey to teenagers
- Concise — students skim on small screens

## Canonical Terminology

| Use This | NOT This |
|----------|----------|
| Operative | Student, player, user |
| Cyber-Flux | Coins, currency, gold |
| Intel Dossier | Grade report, transcript |
| Handler | Teacher (in RPG contexts) |
| XP | Experience points (always abbreviated) |
| Gear / Equipment | Items (for equipped RPG items) |
| Loot | Drops, rewards (for item drops) |
| Mission | Assignment (in RPG contexts) |

In non-RPG instructional contexts (ISLE lessons, physics content), use plain academic language.

## ISLE-to-RPG Narrative Mapping

| ISLE Phase | RPG Framing | Pattern |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| Observational Experiment | Reconnaissance Mission | "Gather intel" — observe, report patterns |
| Explanatory Hypothesis | Mission Briefing | "Develop your theory" — propose explanation |
| Testing Experiment | Infiltration / Boss Encounter | "Test your theory under fire" |
| Application | Field Deployment | "Apply in the real world" |

## Failure Framing
- Never "Wrong answer" or "Try again."
- Frame as actionable intel: "Intel updated — your initial theory didn't hold. Revise your approach, operative."
- Revision = leveling up, not repeating a mistake.

## RPG Content Patterns
- **Loot:** Lead with gameplay effect, then 1 sentence flavor. "+12 Focus, +8 Analysis. Recovered from a deep-cover operative who never came back."
- **Boss encounters:** Pre-battle: 1-2 sentences setting stakes. Victory: celebratory. Defeat: encouraging, never punishing.
- **Empty states:** Frame as opportunity. "No loot yet — complete a dungeon run to earn your first drop."
- **Tooltips:** Max 120 characters, lead with benefit.
