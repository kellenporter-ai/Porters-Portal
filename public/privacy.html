Privacy Policy

Porter's Portal — Perth Amboy High School

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Porter's Portal is an educational platform built for Perth Amboy High School (PAHS) teachers and students. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We are committed to protecting student privacy and complying with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (via Google Authentication)
  • Role (student or teacher/admin)
  • Class enrollments and section assignments

Educational Content & Submissions

  • Assignment responses, essays, and uploaded work
  • Rubric grades and teacher feedback
  • Assessment scores and progress metrics
  • Draft responses (stored locally in your browser until submitted)

Engagement & Telemetry

  • Time spent on assignments and activities
  • Interaction events (clicks, scrolls, block completions)
  • Session activity timestamps

Integrity Analysis: We analyze behavioral patterns (typing consistency, timing) during assessments to flag potential academic integrity concerns. Raw behavioral data is processed in real-time only and is never stored. We retain only a final integrity confidence score and flag status.

Gamification Data

  • Experience points (XP), levels, and achievements
  • Loot inventory and specialization choices
  • Leaderboard standings (pseudonymized for public display)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Educational Delivery: Display assignments, track submissions, and provide feedback
  • Grading: Enable teachers to review, score, and return student work
  • Google Classroom Integration: Push grades to linked Google Classroom assignments (teacher-initiated only)
  • Progress Monitoring: Help teachers identify students who may need additional support
  • Notifications: Email students when new assignments are posted or grades are released
  • Academic Integrity: Detect anomalous submission patterns to maintain fair assessment
  • Platform Improvement: Aggregate, anonymized analytics to improve the learning experience

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore database, Cloud Functions, and Authentication) with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Access is controlled through:

  • Firebase Authentication with role-based access (student vs. admin)
  • Firestore security rules enforcing document-level permissions
  • Students can only read their own submissions and telemetry
  • Teachers can only access submissions from their enrolled classes

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services solely for educational purposes:

  • Google Firebase — Hosting, database, authentication, and cloud functions
  • Google Classroom API — Grade pushing (initiated by teachers with explicit OAuth consent)
  • Google Authentication — Secure sign-in for teachers and students

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties for commercial purposes.

6. FERPA Compliance

Porter's Portal operates under FERPA's "School Official" exception. We act on behalf of Perth Amboy High School to perform educational services that the school would otherwise perform itself. All data collection is limited to what represents a legitimate educational interest.

  • Student educational records are used only for educational purposes
  • We do not send raw student responses to external AI/LLM providers
  • Predictive analytics (e.g., early warning indicators) are used for intervention triggers only — they do not determine final grades

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Educational data is retained while the student is enrolled
  • Graduated/withdrawn students: Data may be retained for one academic year, then deleted or anonymized
  • Transactional data: XP events and engagement logs are subject to automatic time-based cleanup (TTL)
  • Local drafts: Unsubmitted draft responses stored in your browser expire after 7 days

8. Your Rights

For Students & Parents:

  • Right to access your own educational records within the platform
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of data (subject to school record-keeping requirements)
  • Parents may request access to their child's records in accordance with FERPA and district policy

For Teachers:

  • Right to export grade data via CSV
  • Right to unlink Google Classroom integrations at any time
  • Right to request account deletion and associated data removal

9. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to preserve draft feedback and UI preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Firebase Authentication uses session cookies to maintain login state securely.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Material changes will be communicated to teachers and posted on this page with an updated effective date.
