◆ Circuit Schematic 1

Build a circuit with the following requirements:

  • Three light bulbs
  • Two batteries
  • One master switch that turns all three light bulbs on and off together
  • The bulbs must be wired so that unscrewing any one bulb causes all other bulbs to turn off
  • Each light bulb must have a way to be made dimmer and brighter independently

Use a label to mark this as Circuit 1.

◆ Circuit Schematic 2

Build a circuit with the following requirements:

  • Three light bulbs
  • One battery
  • Each light bulb has its own independent switch that turns it on/off without affecting the others
  • One master switch that turns off all light bulbs at once
  • One bulb should be bright (full brightness)
  • One bulb should be dim (reduced brightness)
  • One bulb should be adjustable (can be made dimmer or brighter)

Use a label to mark this as Circuit 2.

How to Use

Place: Click a part, then click the grid.
Move: Select tool, drag parts or labels.
Rotate: Select a part, press R.
Wire: Wire tool, click two points.
Label: Label tool, click to place, double-click to edit.
Delete: Select anything, press Del.
Switch: Click to toggle open/closed.
Auto-save: Your work saves automatically.
